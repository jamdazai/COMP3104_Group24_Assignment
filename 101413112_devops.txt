COMP3104: DevOps Course Overview

COMP3104 is a course designed to introduce students to the principles and practices of DevOps, focusing on the integration of development and operations to enhance collaboration and efficiency throughout the software development lifecycle. Key topics include Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery (CI/CD), Infrastructure as Code (IaC), containerization with Docker and Kubernetes, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure. Students will learn to automate processes, manage infrastructure, and implement monitoring and feedback loops to ensure reliable software releases. The course combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications through case studies and hands-on projects.
